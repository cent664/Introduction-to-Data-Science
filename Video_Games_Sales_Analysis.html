# First group by the data
vdpg = vgs_data_platform.groupby(['Year_of_Release', 'Platform_General','Platform'])
# Second add counts according to group_by info and then join with the statistical information required
counts = vdpg.size().to_frame(name='counts')
vgs_df_pf_grouped = (counts
.join(vdpg.agg({'Global_Sales': 'sum'}).rename(columns={'Global_Sales': 'Global_Sales(M#)'}))

  • Because of PS is the most saled platform around the world, more producers and game companies publish much more games for PS then the Xbox and Nintendo and this result with a lot of games with low sales amounts
